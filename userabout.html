Brainchild Of The Sukma Police,'DONATE A BOOK FOR SUKMA' Is A Humble Initiative Mobilised By The Police,
Administration, Civil Society Members, Press And Some Volunteers.
It Is An Endeavour To Nudge The LWE Affected District Towards A Brighter Future.Focused Primarily On The
Younger Generation, The Intention Is To Enable The Children To Carve A Niche Out For Themselves By Themselves. The Digital
Repository Of Books Is Hoped To Open The Gates To A New World That Awaits Them- A World Of Curiosity, Of Hope, And Of
Light, A World Every Child Deserves.
A Young District, Sukma Turns 9 On 16Th January 2021. Located In South Eastern Chhattisgarh, It Is A Part Of Bastar
Region. The Name 'Bastar' Rings A Bell In Every Mind That Has Ever Heard Of 'Naxals', 'Maoists', 'Left Wing Extremists' In India,
Even Vaguely. Infamous For Conflicts And Tensions, The Region Has Been Disturbed For Most Part Of Its Existence. With 85% Of Its
Population Being Scheduled Tribes (Sts), The Region Has An Unenviable Literacy Rate Of 29% As Mentioned On The District
Website In The Census 2011 Demographic Indicators..
It Is In This Context That The Initiative Has Been Launched As A Small Step Towards A More Peaceful And Progressive
Tomorrow. The Baton Of This Tomorrow Is To Be Held And Led By An Educated, Informed, And Motivated Generation Of Today.
